Launching the Maternal Health Initiative

Pinar Foundation recently launched a new initiative aimed at improving maternal health services in underserved communities. The program focuses on community engagement, health education, and strengthening access to maternal care.


Through collaboration with local partners and health providers, the initiative aims to support safer pregnancies and healthier outcomes for mothers and children.
