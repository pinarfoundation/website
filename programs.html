Our Programs

Pinar Foundation develops programs that strengthen maternal and child health through research, community engagement, and policy advocacy.

Strategic Focus Areas

Maternal Health

Improving access to quality maternal care and supporting safe pregnancy and childbirth.

Child Nutrition

Preventing malnutrition and promoting healthy growth and development for children.

Health Systems Strengthening

Supporting stronger health services and systems to deliver quality care.

Research and Data

Generating evidence to inform health programs and policies.

Key Programs

Maternal Health Initiative

This program focuses on improving maternal health outcomes through community engagement, health education, and collaboration with health providers.

  • Maternal health education programs
  • Strengthening antenatal care awareness
  • Community health outreach
  • Support for maternal health policy dialogue

Child Nutrition Program

The Child Nutrition Program works to prevent malnutrition and promote healthy early childhood development.

  • Community nutrition education
  • Early childhood growth monitoring
  • Stunting prevention initiatives
  • Nutrition awareness campaigns

Research and Knowledge Initiative

Pinar Foundation produces research and knowledge products to support better maternal and child health programs and policies.

  • Policy briefs
  • Research publications
  • Health data analysis
  • Knowledge dissemination

Advocacy and Policy Engagement

The foundation engages with policymakers and stakeholders to strengthen maternal and child health policies.

  • Policy dialogue forums
  • Evidence-based advocacy
  • Stakeholder engagement
  • Public awareness campaigns

Collaborate With Us

We welcome partnerships with researchers, organizations, and communities to expand the impact of our programs.
