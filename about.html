About Pinar Foundation

We are a non-profit organization dedicated to improving maternal and child health through research, innovation, and community empowerment.

Who We Are

Pinar Foundation was established to respond to ongoing challenges in maternal and child health, particularly in communities where access to quality care remains limited.

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

A world where every mother and child lives a healthy and dignified life.

Our Mission

To advance maternal and child health through research, policy advocacy, and community-driven programs.

Our Values

Integrity

We uphold transparency and accountability in all our work.

Equity

We ensure inclusive access to health services for all communities.

Innovation

We embrace research and innovation to drive sustainable change.
