About Pinar Foundation

Advancing maternal and child health through research, innovation, and community action.

Our Story

Pinar Foundation was established with a vision to improve the wellbeing of mothers and children through evidence-based programs and collaborative partnerships. Across many communities, mothers and children still face barriers to accessing quality health services, nutrition, and reliable health information.


Recognizing these challenges, Pinar Foundation works to bridge research, community action, and policy advocacy. By generating data and implementing innovative programs, the foundation aims to support sustainable improvements in maternal and child health outcomes.


Our work focuses on empowering communities, strengthening health systems, and producing research that informs better health policies and programs.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To improve maternal and child health through research, community engagement, and evidence-informed policy advocacy.

Our Vision

A future where every mother and child can live healthy, safe, and thriving lives regardless of their social or economic circumstances.

Our Approach

Research Driven

Producing high-quality evidence to guide health programs and policies.

Community Focused

Working with communities to understand needs and co-create solutions.

Policy Engagement

Supporting policy dialogue to strengthen maternal and child health systems.

Innovation

Using new approaches and technology to improve health outcomes.

Partner With Us

We welcome collaboration with researchers, communities, and partners to advance maternal and child health.
